# SQL | Human Resources Department Project Through SQL

### Goals of Department
+ Culture, Values, Objectives
    + Pay Equity vs Market
    + Employee Demographics eg. Diversity 
    + Headcount Growth vs Balance Sheet
    + Increase Job Satisfaction
    + Transformation Opportunities
+ Decrease Attrition
    + Identifying Data Reasons of Attrition
    + Identifying Data Reasons for Staying
        + Analyzing presence of Benefits
+ Decrease Non-Performers
    + Up-skilling & Training
        + Finding Systemic Faults
    + Layoff
        + Performance Reasons
        + Behavior Reasons eg. Absenteeism
        + Culture Misfit
+ Upraising Best Performers
    + Promotion in current role
    + Upraising to increased responsibility
+ Reduce Costs & Increase Revenue
    + Identifying Management Talent
    + Recruiting in Growth Areas
    + Streamlining Talent Density
